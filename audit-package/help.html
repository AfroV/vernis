Getting Started

Everything you need to know about your Vernis digital art frame

1 First Setup

Option A: Ethernet (Easiest)

  1. Plug ethernet cable into your Vernis
  2. Device automatically connects to your network
  3. IP address appears on the Connect card on home screen

Option B: Bluetooth Setup (Recommended)

  1. On first boot (or when no WiFi), device shows blinking blue LED
  2. On your phone, open setup.html in Chrome
  3. Tap "Connect via Bluetooth" and select Vernis-Setup
  4. Enter your WiFi network name and password
  5. Tap Connect — device joins your network automatically
  6. Note the IP address shown on success
Requires Chrome/Edge

Web Bluetooth only works in Chrome, Edge, or Opera browsers. Safari and Firefox are not supported.

Option C: WiFi Hotspot (Fallback)

  1. If Bluetooth fails, device creates hotspot: Vernis-Setup
  2. Connect your phone or laptop to this hotspot
  3. Open browser and go to http://192.168.4.1
  4. Select your WiFi network and enter password
  5. Device reboots and connects to your network

Bluetooth Setup (Recommended)

  1. Power on your Vernis Petit
  2. Wait for blinking blue LED (setup mode)
  3. On your phone, open vernis.io/setup in Chrome
  4. Tap "Connect via Bluetooth" and select Vernis-Setup
  5. Enter your WiFi network name and password
  6. Note the IP address shown on success
  7. Solid green LED = connected successfully

Hotspot Fallback

  1. If Bluetooth isn't available, connect to hotspot: Vernis-Setup
  2. Open browser and go to http://192.168.4.1
  3. Enter WiFi credentials and connect
Tip

Write down the IP address! You'll need it to access your Petit from other devices.

23 Getting Art Collections

  1. Navigate to Library (or go to http://[ip]/library.html)
  2. Browse available collections from GitHub and local storage
  3. Each card shows: Collection name, NFT count, and download progress

34 Downloading & Pinning

  1. Find a collection you want (e.g., "AfroViking’s DOOM Party")
  2. Tap "Install"
  3. Watch the progress bar fill blue as files download
  4. Bar turns green when complete
Auto-Pin Enabled

Files are automatically pinned to IPFS as they download, ensuring your art is preserved on the decentralized network.

45 Viewing Your Art

  1. Tap "Fullscreen Gallery" on home screen
  2. Art cycles automatically in beautiful fullscreen mode
  3. Tap screen to show controls: Next, Previous, Pause, Exit

Remote Control

Control your gallery from your phone:

http://[device-ip]/remote.html

With HDMI Display

  1. Connect HDMI cable to your TV or monitor
  2. From your phone, open http://[ip]/remote.html
  3. Tap "Start Gallery"
  4. Control playback from your phone

56 Backup to Computer

  1. Go to Settings (http://[ip]/settings.html)
  2. Scroll to Storage & Backup section
  3. Click "Download Backup"
  4. Save the ZIP file to your Mac or PC

The backup contains all your pinned artwork files that can be restored later.

67 Network Changed?

If you change your WiFi network or move locations:

Method 1: Bluetooth (Easiest)

  1. When WiFi is unavailable, device automatically enters Bluetooth setup mode
  2. Open setup.html in Chrome on your phone
  3. Connect and enter new WiFi credentials

Method 2: With Ethernet

  1. Connect ethernet cable temporarily
  2. Go to Settings → WiFi
  3. Select new network and enter password
  4. Remove ethernet cable

Method 3: Hotspot Fallback

  1. After ~90 seconds without WiFi, device creates hotspot
  2. Connect to Vernis-Setup hotspot
  3. Configure new WiFi at http://192.168.4.1

Method 1: Bluetooth (Easiest)

  1. When WiFi is unavailable, LED turns blinking blue
  2. Open vernis.io/setup in Chrome
  3. Connect via Bluetooth and enter new credentials

Method 2: Ethernet

  1. Plug ethernet into Petit
  2. Find new IP from your router's admin page
  3. Access http://[new-ip]/settings.html
  4. Configure new WiFi

  2. Connect and configure at http://192.168.4.1

78 Software Updates

  1. Go to Settings → System
  2. Click "Check for Updates"
  3. If available, click "Install Update"
  4. Device will reboot with the new version

Alternative: USB Update

  1. Download update file from vernis.io
  2. Copy to USB drive (FAT32 formatted)
  3. Insert USB into Petit
  4. Hold button for 3 seconds → Update installs automatically
