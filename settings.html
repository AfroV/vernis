Why HTTPS?

            

              HTTPS encrypts data between your browser and Vernis. This is
              optional for home use but recommended if accessing Vernis from
              outside your network.
            


            

              Note:
              Self-signed certificates will show a browser warning. This is
                normal for home setups.
